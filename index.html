Welcome

Welcome to my personal website! I am Jyoti Ranjan Behera, a Ph.D. graduate from the Department of Biological Sciences at East Tennessee State University. My research focuses on the role of avocado WRINKLED proteins in oil content and composition. Explore my website to learn more about my research, publications, and professional activities.

About Me

I am a dedicated researcher in the field of Biomedical Sciences with a Ph.D. from East Tennessee State University. My research primarily focuses on elucidating the role of avocado WRINKLED proteins in oil biosynthesis. I have a strong background in biological sciences, having completed an integrated BS-MS from the National Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhubaneswar, India. My passion for plant science has driven me to explore various facets of lipid metabolism, stress resistance in plants, and innovative biotechnological applications.
